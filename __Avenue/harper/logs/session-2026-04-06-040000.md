# Session Log - 2026-04-06 04:00:00

## What Was Wrong / What Was Requested

The **Figure 6** (mean blood lead) block showed **“Figure 4: *”** (or similar) above the real sources paragraph while the plot title correctly said **Figure 6**. This comes from **`\caption*`** still going through caption machinery that prepends **`Figure~\thefigure`**, and the **figure counter** does not match the document’s **manual** figure titles (1–9).

## How I Fixed It / What I Did

In `Lead, Highways, and Systemic Harm.tex`, **removed** `\caption*{...}` under the Figure 6 `tikzpicture` and replaced it with the same pattern as Figures 4–5:

- `\\[0.4em]`
- `\parbox{\linewidth}{\raggedright\small\emph{Sources: ...}}`

No `\caption*`, so **no automatic figure number** is injected. The **Figure 6** label remains only in the **`pgfplots` title** (`Figure 6: Mean Blood Lead Levels...`).
