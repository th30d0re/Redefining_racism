## Challenges Encountered

1. Statute numbers as years: "92 Stat. 1783" → year=1783. Fixed by author-field correction (legislation entries get `{U.S. Congress}` author), and validation step flags year < 1900 for @misc legislation entries.
2. Multi-author formatting: "Last, F., and Last2, F2." split algorithm misclassified some entries because threshold was too loose (≤ 25 chars). Added double-brace protection as fallback.
3. Legal case entries: "Unknown" author is not useful. Replaced with `{{Supreme Court of the United States}}` for SCOTUS cases.
