## Challenges Encountered

1. **CSA subsection format mismatch:** The Controlled Substances Act (21 USC §812) uses `**(b) Heading text**` for top-level subsections, while most Title 18 sections use `**(b)**`. The extraction function matched a sub-item `**(b)**` deep in the drug schedules instead of the top-level subsection heading. Fixed by changing the start-pattern match from `**(X)**` to `**(X)` (removing the trailing bold close), which correctly matches both formats.

2. **Chinese Exclusion content in statutory notes:** The Chinese Exclusion chapter (8 USC ch. 7) has all meaningful content in `### Statutory Notes` sections, since every section is marked "Repealed" or "Omitted." The standard `section_to_notes` mode would strip this content. Added `section_with_notes` mode to preserve it.

3. **Markdown h3 headings not rendered:** The `### Statutory Notes` heading in the Chinese Exclusion excerpt was being escaped as `\#\#\# Statutory Notes` instead of rendered as a LaTeX heading. Added `### ` handling to the `md_to_latex()` converter.
