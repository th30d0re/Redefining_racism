## How I Fixed It / What I Did

2. **Replaced the composite formula**: Changed from implicit `blocked_count / 3` to the explicit continuous form `E_score = (1/3) Σ e_i`.

4. **Updated the Total Enclosure equation**: From `3/3 = 1.0` to `(1/3)(1+1+1) = 1.0` with explicit `e_1 = e_2 = e_3 = 1` condition.

5. **Made the 0.8 claim mathematically traceable**: The prose now specifies "reducing e_2 from 1.0 to 0.4" while "e_1 = e_3 = 1", which yields `(1/3)(1.0 + 0.4 + 1.0) = 0.8` — a verifiable calculation under the new formula.
