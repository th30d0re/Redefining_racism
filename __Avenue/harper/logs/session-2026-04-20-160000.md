## Challenges Encountered

1. **pgfplots thousands separator**: pgfplots applies `/pgf/number format` by default to tick values, inserting commas. Using explicit `xticklabels={...}` bypasses all formatting and guarantees clean display.
2. **Anchor semantics with rotate=90**: With `rotate=90, anchor=south`, the BOTTOM of the vertical text is anchored at the coordinate. y=3 is technically inside the chart (ymin=0) but appears visually low because rotated tick labels take up below-axis space and the eye reads the label base as near the axis.
3. **Right-side congestion**: Three separate annotations (Gilded Age line label, 2020 arrow+text, Kernel Recompile subtitle) all converged in the x=1990–2022, y=25–31 zone. Moving the Gilded Age label to the left side removed the most crowded element from that zone.
